about us

Symposium made extra fun!

ELECTROSPITZER 2K22 is one of the most awaited and prestigious event of the Department Of Electronics adn Communication Engineering in Anna University Regional Campus, Tirunelveli
The main objective is to bring students together from different colleges across regional level to share the knowledge. this event provides a platform for young minds to be the technology changer with their great ideas and showcase their knowledge in a healthy way.
"All of us do not have equal talent. But, all of us have an oppurtunity to develop our talents."
-Dr. A. P. J Abdul Kalam
Inspired by the above beautiful quote, ELECTROSPITZER provides you the golden oppurtunity to showcase your talents in various technical and non-technical events. It will be an all inclusive package for learning, fun and much more awaits! we look forward to see you!

Save the date!

Non-Tech / Fun Events

Secret Stash

Alert..!! To all the Pirates out there !
An invitation for the biggest treasure hunt ,Grab your mates and join the event.

This is an non-technical event, the idea of finding treasure has always been exciting and fun.
The participants work in a small team and find the treasure with the help of the given clues.

Guidelines and rules:
1. Each team should consist of three participants.
2. Clues of the treasure will be placed inside the campus.

Lumosity

"Open your eyes, look at screen & sharpen your brain." Not only software has memory, Human also, but how it's working? Are you ready to check?

This is non-technical & brainy event, to check your memory or increase your memory power with this exciting game. A slide of pictures will be presented and the participants should write the pictures what they have seen in correct order.

Guidelines and Rules:
1. Two members can participate in one team.
2. Event comprises of two rounds.

Tie-In

Train your brain, have fun and be inspired!
CONNECT.. CONNECT.. CONNECT yaarrrr....
Watch out , Train your brain, connect it , Have fun and know your hidden talent.

Tie up is an non technical event. Sequence of pictures will be displayed in a slide. Groups will be finding the opt word by connecting the pictures.

Guidelines and rules:
1. Each team should consist of three participants.
2. Candidates who clear the Pen and Paper round will be participating in a buzzer round.

Exquiz Me

Knowledge becomes power only when we put it into some use...!!
So let's honour our knowledge by answering some simple GK questions.

This is a non-technicale event. Questions will be mostly on general topics like social, science, non-verbal reasoning, important days and sports.

Guidelines and rules:
1. Candidates who clear the MCQ round will be participating in a buzzer round.
2. Only single participation is allowed.

Dumbcharads

"Our students have acting capability! Do you have ability to find out? "

This is a fun event. Dumb charads is a game where in a person enacts the words and the groups have to guess it. The person enacting cannot speak or point to a particular object.

Guidelines and Rules:
1.The group includes only 2 participants.
2.Event comprises of two rounds.
Round 1: Words and sentence based questions.
Round 2: Picture based questions.

Adfab

" Engineers have all talent".
You got this.. Get on to your marketing mode.
Grab this amazing opportunity to prove your marketing skill!

This event is developing a themed exhibit, display, or a presentation to promote a product or service.
Participants should advertise the products given to them.

Guidelines and rules:
1. Single round event.
2. Participants will be an individual or group.
